ABOUT

Ripples Boutique

               Welcome to Ripples Boutique, where passion meets craftsmanship. Founded in 1997 by Sajani Aiyappa, Ripples Boutique has grown from a cherished dream into an exquisite destination of handmade embroidery. Sajani's journey began with a simple love for needle and thread, a love that has transformed into a thriving boutique known for its dedication to quality and artistry. At Ripples Boutique, we specialize in creating beautiful, handcrafted embroidery products that add a touch of elegance to everyday life. Each product is carefully designed and embroidered, reflecting our commitment to quality and the joy of handmade craftsmanship. We take pride in offering unique, personalized items that cater to babies, young girls, and ladies, ensuring there's something special for everyone. Thank you for visiting Ripples Boutique. We invite you to explore our collection and discover the beauty and elegance of handcrafted embroidery. Your satisfaction is our inspiration, and we are honored to share our passion with you.

Our collection includes: Frocks: Delicate and charming designs perfect for babies and young girls. Nighties: Comfortable and stylish nightwear for ladies. Home decor and furnish : Elevate your home décor with our intricately designed pieces. And many more products
